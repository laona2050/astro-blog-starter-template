    <p style="color: #6b7280; margin-bottom: 2rem;">
      刚性太阳能电池板是最传统的太阳能发电产品，采用钢化玻璃面板保护，适用于固定安装场景。
    </p>

        <div class="data-point"><strong>重量：</strong>10-12kg/m²，厚度17-25mm<a href="#" class="citation">[26]</a></div>

        <div class="data-point"><strong>温度系数：</strong>-0.4%/K（温度每升高1°C，效率下降0.4%）</div>
      </div>
      <div class="comparison-card">
        <h4 style="color: #1f2937;">多晶硅太阳能电池板</h4>
        <div class="data-point"><strong>转换效率：</strong>15%-18%，较单晶硅低3-5个百分点</div>
        <div class="data-point"><strong>成本优势：</strong>制造成本降低10%-20%</div>

    <p style="color: #6b7280; margin-bottom: 2rem;">
      柔性太阳能电池板采用薄膜封装技术，可弯曲贴合曲面，应用场景更灵活。
    </p>
    
    <div class="grid-2 mb-8">
      <div class="comparison-card">
        <h4 style="color: #1f2937;">高效单晶柔性板（SunPower电池片）</h4>
        <div class="data-point"><strong>核心电池：</strong>SunPower Maxeon系列背接触（IBC）电池<a href="#" class="citation">[38]</a></div>
        <div class="data-point"><strong>转换效率：</strong>22.4%-23.7%（量产最高水平）</div>
        <div class="data-point"><strong>封装材料：</strong>ETFE薄膜，透光率>95%</div>
        <div class="data-point"><strong>重量厚度：</strong>2-3kg/m²，厚度仅2-3mm</div>
        <div class="data-point"><strong>弯曲性能：</strong>最大弯曲角度30°，最小弯曲半径约500mm</div>
        <div class="data-point"><strong>功率范围：</strong>50W-300W/块</div>
      </div>
      <div class="comparison-card">
        <h4 style="color: #1f2937;">薄膜柔性太阳能电池板</h4>
        <div class="data-point"><strong>技术路线：</strong>非晶硅（a-Si）、CIGS、CdTe等</div>
        <div class="data-point"><strong>转换效率：</strong>CIGS为15%-17%，非晶硅为6%-9%<a href="#" class="citation">[39]</a></div>
        <div class="data-point"><strong>物理特性：</strong>厚度小于 1mm，重量小于 1kg/m²，可卷曲</div>
        <div class="data-point"><strong>弱光性能：</strong>优异，温度系数优越（-0.2%/K）</div>
        <div class="data-point"><strong>光致衰减：</strong>非晶硅存在15%-25%稳定效率损失<a href="#" class="citation">[32]</a></div>
        <div class="data-point"><strong>颜色外观：</strong>深色/黑色，美观度高</div>
      </div>
    </div>
